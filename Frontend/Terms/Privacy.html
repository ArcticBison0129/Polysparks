1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how polysparks ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information through our AI tool discovery platform (the "Platform"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

By using the Platform, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address, and other account registration details
  • AI tool submissions and reviews
  • Communications with our team, including support requests
  • Survey responses and feedback

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, clickstream data
  • Search queries and filter preferences
  • Tool interactions and usage patterns

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

  • We use cookies to personalize your experience, improve the Platform, and analyze traffic
  • Analytics cookies help us understand user behavior and improve our services
  • You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the polysparks platform
  • Provide personalized AI tool recommendations
  • Improve content, layout, and user experience
  • Communicate with users about updates, new tools, or support
  • Monitor usage trends and detect fraud or misuse
  • Process tool submissions and maintain our directory
  • Send relevant newsletters and product updates (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email services)
  • AI tool providers when you interact with their tools through our platform
  • Government agencies or legal entities when required by law
  • Parties involved in a corporate transaction (e.g., merger or acquisition)

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The Platform may link to or embed third-party AI tools and services. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content, practices, or security.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party AI tools they interact with through our platform.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure data storage and backup procedures

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information transmitted to or stored on the Platform.

7. International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer and processing.

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for European users.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13.

If we learn we have inadvertently collected such data, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians who believe we have collected information from a child under 13 should contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

You may have certain rights depending on your jurisdiction, including:

  • Accessing your personal information
  • Requesting correction of inaccurate data
  • Requesting deletion of your data
  • Objecting to certain data uses
  • Withdrawing consent for data processing
  • Data portability (receiving your data in a machine-readable format)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@polysparks.com

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.

We will notify you of significant changes and revise the effective date at the top. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@polysparks.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days of receipt.
