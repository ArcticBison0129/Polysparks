Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an AI tool to your directory?
You can submit your AI tool by clicking the "Submit Tool" button on the website. Fill out the simple form with your tool's details, and we'll review it within 1-3 business days. No fees or technical setup are required for basic submissions.
Is it free to list my AI tool?
Yes! Submitting and listing your AI tool is completely free. We believe in making AI discovery accessible to everyone. While we may offer optional premium placements in the future, basic listings will always remain free.
How long does it take to review submissions?
Most submissions are reviewed and published within 1-3 business days. We manually verify each tool to ensure quality and accuracy. You'll receive a confirmation email once your tool is live on the platform.
Can I update my tool's information after it's listed?
Absolutely! You can request updates anytime by contacting us through this form or emailing us directly. We're happy to help keep your tool's information accurate, up-to-date, and relevant to users.
Do I need an account to browse or submit tools?
No account is required! Everything is open and accessible to everyone. You can browse, search, and submit without any registration. We believe in keeping things simple and barrier-free.
What makes a good AI tool submission?
Great submissions include clear descriptions, accurate categorization, working links, and honest information about pricing and features. Tools that solve real problems and provide genuine value to users tend to perform best in our directory.
